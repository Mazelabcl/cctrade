Entry & Exit Criteria Used (Run #1: Baseline)
Entry Rule (all levels)

For each naked level (not previously touched):

  • LONG (bounce from support): Candle low touches level (within 2% tolerance) AND candle closes ABOVE the level.
  • SHORT (rejection from resistance): Candle high touches level (within 2% tolerance) AND candle closes BELOW the level.

The close confirmation is key: it proves the level held, not just that price touched it.

Exit Rules
  • Take Profit: +4% from entry price
  • Stop Loss: -2% from entry price (R:R = 2:1)
  • Timeout: 50 candles (if neither TP nor SL hit)

Strategy: fixed_percent — SL and TP as fixed % of entry.

Next Iterations

This is the baseline. We plan to also test:

  • ATR-based: SL/TP dynamic based on volatility
  • Level-based: TP at next level, SL at opposite level
  • Different tolerances: 1%, 2%, 3%
  • Different R:R ratios: 1:1, 2:1, 3:1
